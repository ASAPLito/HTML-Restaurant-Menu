Origin Story

The first H-Thai-M-L restaurant was started in 1988 at 2872 Pine Lane by Somchai Chen. It became so successful that Mr. Chen opened locations on Braodway Street, Bacon Race Road, and Sugar Drive. Now, Mr. Chen is retired from cooking, but you can still find him in his restaurants tasting the food to make sure it's still good.
